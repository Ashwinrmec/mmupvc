Who we are

LESSO Group, started in 1986, is world-wide leading industrial group of home building material, listed in Hong Kong Stock Exchange (stock code: 2128). The range of products covers UPVC Profile, UPVC plumbing products, sanitary ware, CP fittings, modular kitchen, decorative plates, fire-fighting equipments, water purifiers, electrical switches & sockets, wooden doors and etc. Ranking among the toppest in UPVC pipe manufacturers in the world, the sales revenue in 2016 is over 16,000 Crore Indian Rupees.

LESSO Buildtech Pvt. Ltd, based in Gurgaon, India, is a wholly owned subsidiary of LESSO Group represented internationally.
